Mediterranean Pizza

This Mediterranean Pizza is perfect as a healthy weeknight dinner!

There’s nothing like pizza for dinner. Especially when it’s a wholesome, flavor-loaded recipe like this Mediterranean Pizza. Use a pre-made pizza crust or make your own vegan pizza dough for an easy, 30-minute recipe that everyone will love!

Igredients

  • Pizza Dough
  • Garlic
  • Mozzarella + Feta
  • Spinach
  • Grape Tomatoes
  • Black Olives
  • Artichokes
  • Red Onion
  • Parsley

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F.
  2. Make the pizza dough and bake for about 8 minutes.
  3. Drizzle the olive oil over the pizza.
  4. Top with minced garlic, mozzarella, spinach, tomatoes, olives, artichokes, red onion slices, and crumbled feta.
  5. Add fresh or dried parsley on top.
  6. Bake for about 15-20 minutes, or until the vegetables begin to soften.
  7. Let cool, slice, and enjoy!
Notes

To Store: Allow the pizza to fully cool, then store the slices in an airtight container. Add the lid and keep in the fridge for 3-5 days and the freezer for up to 3 months!
